Remix

This is my Remix art. Using Adobe Photoshop, I edited the heads of sheep and wolves onto the bodies of people. You may have notcied that the sheep are women and the wolves are men. I wanted to portray how women may feel when they are walking alone and how they fear of being preyed upon by a man. As a young woman myself, I completely understand this feeling so I wanted to try and express that feeling into a visual image that others can see and perhaps understand it or learn something new.
