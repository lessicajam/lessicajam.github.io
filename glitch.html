Glitch

This is my Glitch art piece. Using Textedit, I took an image of a dog and simply added in and removed text from the image's code in order to force it to glitch. I saved the image everytime it changed significantly. After collecting quite a few images of the glitch, I made a gif of it on Adobe Premier. There's not really any deep message that I was trying to convey here. This was simply a fun activty for me to do.
